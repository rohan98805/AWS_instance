About LandSmart

LandSmart is a smarter land management platform that helps buyers and sellers transact securely. Verified sellers can post property details for admin approval, while verified buyers can compare prices and message sellers directly. The platform ensures verified users, safe transactions, and a transparent marketplace for land records and properties.
